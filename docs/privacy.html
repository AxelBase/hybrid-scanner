Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the AxelBase Hybrid Scanner, a fully client-side web application provided by AxelBase ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for this privacy-focused tool that decrypts hybrid QR and barcode combinations directly in your browser. We prioritize your privacy through a zero-data-collection model. All scanning and decryption occur locally on your device, and no personal information is collected, stored, or shared. This policy provides full transparency about our approach.

2. No Data Collection

The Hybrid Scanner is engineered to operate without accessing or transmitting any user data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers: Names, email addresses, IP addresses, or device details.
  • Scanned content: Decrypted secrets, camera feeds, or any QR/barcode data remain exclusively on your device.
  • Usage metrics: No analytics, tracking pixels, cookies, or session logs are implemented.
  • Location or behavioral data: Nothing related to your browsing habits or geographic position.

Since processing happens entirely client-side using libraries like CryptoJS, jsQR, and ZXing, your information never reaches our servers or third parties.

3. How the Scanner Operates

When using the AxelBase Hybrid Scanner:

  • Camera access: Granted temporarily for live scanning and revoked when you disable it or close the page.
  • Local computation: Encryption keys, salts, and decryption run in your browser's memory only.
  • Exports: Copy or download features use native browser functions without server involvement.

Temporary data may reside in browser memory during use but clears automatically upon page refresh or closure. We implement no persistent storage mechanisms.

4. Third-Party Libraries

We utilize open-source libraries loaded via CDNs or bundled locally. These do not track users or send data back to their providers in this context.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The scanner uses no cookies, localStorage for persistence, or any tracking technologies. Sessions are completely stateless.

6. Security Considerations

While we collect nothing, users should:

  • Use updated browsers on secure devices.
  • Handle exported plaintext responsibly.
  • Revoke camera permissions when finished.

7. Children's Privacy

This tool is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly process their data.

8. International Compliance

Our no-collection model aligns with GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws by design.

9. Policy Updates

Changes will appear here with updated effective dates.

10. Contact

For questions, reach us via the contact section on our site.

Your privacy is protected by design—nothing leaves your device.
